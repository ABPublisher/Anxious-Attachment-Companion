Cashflow Forecast Budget (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

Cashflow Forecast Budget is a personal finance planning tool that allows users to manually enter financial information such as transactions, bills, and budgeting data. The App does not connect to bank accounts and does not provide financial, investment, or tax advice.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

What This Policy Covers

  • What information is collected
  • How information is stored
  • Whether information is shared
  • Your rights and choices regarding your data

Important Notice

The App stores financial data locally on your device. The developer does not have access to your financial records.

Information We Collect

Cashflow Forecast Budget collects only the information that you voluntarily provide within the App.

Financial Data (Stored Locally)

The App allows you to manually enter:

  • Transaction amounts, dates, categories, and notes
  • Bill names, due dates, and recurring schedules
  • Budget goals and planning data
  • Currency preference and payday settings

This information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to the developer.

Subscription Information

If you choose to purchase a premium subscription, payment processing is handled securely by Google Play.

The App does not collect or store your credit card details. Subscription status information may be received from Google Play to unlock premium features.

For more information on how Google handles payment data, please refer to Google Play’s Privacy Policy.

Device Information

The App may collect limited technical information necessary for app functionality, such as:

  • App version
  • Device operating system version

This information is used solely to maintain app performance and stability.

Data Storage and Security

All financial data entered into Cashflow Forecast Budget is stored locally on your device.

The developer does not collect, store, transmit, or have access to your financial records.

The App does not use remote servers to store personal financial data in the current version.

While the App is designed with reasonable security measures, you are responsible for securing your device with appropriate protections such as a device passcode or biometric authentication.

If you uninstall the App, locally stored data may be permanently deleted unless backed up by your device’s operating system.

Data Sharing

Cashflow Forecast Budget does not sell, rent, or share your personal financial information with third parties.

Because financial data is stored locally on your device, it is not transmitted to the developer.

The only third-party service involved in the App is Google Play, which may process subscription payments if you choose to purchase premium features. Google Play handles payment information in accordance with its own Privacy Policy.

The App does not use advertising networks and does not share financial data with analytics providers in the current version.

Data Retention

Because Cashflow Forecast Budget stores financial data locally on your device, the App does not retain copies of your financial records on external servers.

Your data remains on your device until:

  • You manually delete it within the App (if applicable), or
  • You uninstall the App from your device.

Uninstalling the App may permanently remove locally stored data unless your device operating system performs its own backup.

The developer does not maintain independent backups of your financial data.

Your Rights and Data Deletion

Because Cashflow Forecast Budget stores data locally on your device, you maintain full control over your financial information.

You may:

  • Edit or delete transactions and bills within the App
  • Remove all stored data by uninstalling the App
  • Stop using the App at any time

The developer does not have access to or control over your locally stored financial data.

If you have questions about how data is handled, you may contact the developer using the information provided below.

Children’s Privacy

Cashflow Forecast Budget is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because all financial data is stored locally on the user’s device and not transmitted to the developer, the developer does not have access to user-entered data.

If you believe a child has provided personal information within the App, you may uninstall the App to remove locally stored data.

Financial Disclaimer

Cashflow Forecast Budget is a personal finance planning tool designed to help users organize and forecast their financial information.

The App does not provide financial, investment, tax, legal, or accounting advice.

Any forecasts, projections, or “safe-to-spend” calculations are estimates based on user-entered data and may not reflect actual future balances.

You are solely responsible for financial decisions made using information provided by the App.
