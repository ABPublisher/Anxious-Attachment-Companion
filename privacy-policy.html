Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 20, 2026

Anxious Attachment Companion (“the App”) is a self-guided emotional wellness application developed by AB Publisher LLC.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data.

All information you enter into the App (including mood ratings, anxiety ratings, journal entries, calm session notes, and reflections) is stored locally on your device only.

We do not have access to your personal entries.

Local Data Storage

The App stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Daily mood and anxiety check-ins
  • Journal entries
  • Calm session notes and ratings
  • Premium entitlement status (stored locally after purchase or restore)

If you uninstall the App, this locally stored data may be permanently deleted.

In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time premium unlock through Google Play Billing.

All payments are processed securely by Google Play. We do not collect or store your payment information.

Google may collect and process payment data according to its own Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

The App uses:

  • Google Play Billing for processing in-app purchases

The App does not use:

  • Advertising SDKs
  • Third-party analytics
  • Tracking technologies

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Because all journal and check-in data is stored locally on your device, it is not transmitted to our servers.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Security

Since all personal content is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device’s security settings.

We recommend using device-level protections such as screen locks or biometric authentication.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
